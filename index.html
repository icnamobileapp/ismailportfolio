Featured case study

NestIQ: one digital pathway for UK residential transactions.

NestIQ sits between passive property portals and traditional high-fee agents. It combines AI-led matching, digital referencing, contract execution and operational tools in one platform for landlords, sellers, tenants and buyers.

Smart properties. Better deals. One platform.

The proposed platform reduces intermediary friction while giving users a clearer, more transparent transaction journey.

01 · THE PROBLEM

High fees, slow workflows and weak matching.

Traditional agency models are costly, while listing portals often stop at discovery. NestIQ targets the gap between visibility and full transaction completion.

02 · THE SOLUTION

AI matching with digital completion.

A landlord dashboard, ranked recommendations, referencing and digital agreements create a more connected end-to-end workflow.

03 · THE MODEL

Recurring revenue plus transaction upside.

Rental subscriptions provide predictability, while sales success fees, referencing and partner referrals create additional revenue.
